# CORA Contributor License Agreement

CORA accepts contributions under a Contributor License Agreement based on the Apache 2.0 Individual CLA.

The CLA grants CORA a license to redistribute the contribution under Apache 2.0 (for code) and CC BY 4.0 (for documentation, crosswalks, skills, and the drift register). It does not assign copyright.

The full CLA text and signing workflow will be published once non-Cherre contributors start arriving. Until then, contributions accepted via pull request carry the implicit understanding that the contributor agrees to these terms.
